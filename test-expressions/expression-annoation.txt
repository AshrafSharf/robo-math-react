M = write(4, 14, "\frac{a}{b} + c = d")
T = subonly(M,"\frac{a}{b}")
cancel(T, "0", "u")

  Or with multiple items:
  M = write(4, 14, "x^2 + 2x + 1")
  T = subonly(M, "x")
  cancel(T, "", "u", "red")

  This will cancel all occurrences of "x" in the expression.

  More examples:
  // Cancel with replacement text
  M = write(4, 14, "\frac{5}{10}")
  T = subonly(M, "5")
  cancel(T, "1", "u")

  // Down diagonal cancel
  M = write(4, 14, "a + b - c")
  T = subonly(M, "b")
  cancel(T, "", "d")

  // X cancel
  M = write(4, 14, "wrong answer")
  T = subonly(M, "wrong")
  cancel(T, "", "x", "red")
